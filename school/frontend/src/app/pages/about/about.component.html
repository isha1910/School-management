Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 1995, D.P. Derasari School started with a simple vision — to create a school where every child matters. Over the years, we have grown from a small community school into one of the most respected educational institutions in the region.

Our journey has been marked by countless achievements, dedicated teachers, and thousands of students who have gone on to excel in various fields across the globe.
